An AI Agent is like a smart virtual employee that works for your business 24 hours a day.

  • Book appointments
  • Generate leads
  • Qualify customers
  • Respond to Facebook, Instagram, and website messages
  • Follow up with customers automatically

Unlike a normal chatbot, an AI Agent understands conversations, remembers context, and provides intelligent responses just like a real assistant.
